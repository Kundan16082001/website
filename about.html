About iWord Technologies

Our Story

iWord Technologies Private Limited was founded in 2020 with a vision to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. Based in Nagpur, Maharashtra, we have grown to become a leading provider of remote IT internships and training programs across India.

Our mission is to empower students and fresh graduates with practical, industry-relevant skills that prepare them for successful careers in technology. We believe in making quality IT education accessible to everyone, regardless of their geographical location.
